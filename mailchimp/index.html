THE DNA FOUNDATION

Thank you for your support of the DNA Foundation over the past several years.

We're excited to announce the next phase of our work as we unveil a new name and new focus for the organization.During the past three years, it’s become clear that technology is playing an increasingly prominent role in child sex trafficking and other forms of child sexual exploitation. Yet the resources and expertise needed to combat this issue are not keeping pace. We want to change that. That’s why we’ve decided to focus our resources on the technical side of our work and invest in technological innovations that disrupt and deflate the predatory behaviors that drive exploitation.

To reflect this new focus, The DNA Foundation is now Thorn: Digital Defenders of Children. We're continuing to work collaboratively with NGOs, law enforcement, government and the private sector to create a comprehensive response to this issue and believe this new focus will bring new expertise and solutions that are sorely missing to this fight.
